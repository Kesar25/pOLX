About Us

Welcome to pOLX, your one-stop destination for buying and selling pre-owned items. Created by a passionate student, pOLX aims to provide a platform for hostellers to sell their belongings when they leave colleges.

Our mission is to make the process of buying and selling old items hassle-free and convenient. Whether you're looking for furniture, electronics, or any other item, pOLX has got you covered!

Explore our website to find amazing deals on a wide range of items. Join the pOLX community today!
